Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Surf Hero?

Surf Hero is a macOS status bar app that allows you to quickly and easily change your default web browser with just a few clicks. Whether you're switching between browsers for work or personal use, Surf Hero streamlines the process, saving you time and hassle.

How do I change my default browser using Surf Hero?

To change your default browser, simply click on the Surf Hero icon in your macOS status bar, select your preferred browser from the list, and Surf Hero will ask macOS to set it as your new default. It's that simple!

Which browsers are supported by Surf Hero?

By default, Surf Hero supports all modern browsers. In case it isn't working with your favorite one please, let us now using our Support page.

Is Surf Hero compatible with the latest macOS versions?

Yes, Surf Hero is fully compatible with macOS Ventura and later versions. Make sure you’re running the latest version of macOS for the best experience.

Does Surf Hero require any special permissions or settings to work?

No, Surf Hero does not require any special permissions or settings to work. The app simply allows you to change your default browser with a few clicks. There is no need to grant additional system access or permissions for Surf Hero to function.

How do I install Surf Hero?

You can download Surf Hero directly from the Mac App Store. After installation, simply open the app, and it will appear in your macOS status bar.

Can I customize Surf Hero's appearance or behavior?

Currently, Surf Hero has a simple and clean interface that integrates seamlessly with macOS. We plan to include more customization options in future updates.

How do I contact Surf Hero support?

If you have any questions or need assistance, please reach out to us using our Support page. We're happy to help!

Can Surf Hero improve my productivity?

Absolutely! By making it easier to switch between browsers, Surf Hero helps you save time when working with multiple browsers, improving your overall workflow and productivity.

Does Surf Hero have any known issues or bugs?

We work hard to maintain a smooth and bug-free experience. However, if you encounter any issues, please check our support page for troubleshooting tips or contact us directly for assistance.

Will Surf Hero be updated regularly?

Yes, we release regular updates to improve functionality, add new features, and ensure compatibility with the latest macOS updates.
